## Overview

The Management View gives you a complete picture of your commercial team's performance. It's designed for sales managers who need to identify trends, compare salespeople, and make coaching decisions.

<Note>
  This view provides the full team ranking and activity heatmap — perfect for weekly sales meetings and performance reviews.
</Note>

## Key Metrics

The top KPI cards provide team-level aggregates:

### Total Sales
- Aggregated revenue across all salespeople
- Period-over-period comparison percentage
- Team objective amount and fulfillment %
- Calculated using `kpi_ventas_periodo(df, df_ant, obj_df, fecha_desde, fecha_hasta)`

### Average Ticket
- Mean transaction value: `total_revenue / transaction_count`
- Helps identify if growth is driven by volume or ticket size

### Salespeople On Target
- Count of salespeople with ≥90% quota achievement
- Shows as number + visual indicator
- Fulfillment calculation uses `label_semaforo == "success"` flag

### Transaction Volume
- Total number of sales transactions processed
- Indicates overall commercial activity level

## Monthly Evolution Chart

The evolution chart displays:

- **Blue line:** Actual team sales by month
- **Red dotted line:** Team objective targets
- **Interactive hover:** Shows exact amounts, transaction count, and fulfillment %

**Calculated using** `kpi_evolucion_mensual(df, obj_df)` which:
1. Groups transactions by month: `df.groupby('fecha').resample('M')`
2. Sums revenue per month
3. Joins with monthly objectives from `objetivos_df`
4. Returns time series ready for Plotly visualization

## Fulfillment Distribution Chart

A horizontal bar chart shows each salesperson's objective achievement:

- Bar length represents fulfillment percentage
- Color coding matches traffic light system
- Sorted by fulfillment % (highest to lowest)
- Threshold line at 100% marks quota achievement

**Generated by** `chart_barras_vendedores(kv_df)` using the salesperson KPI dataframe.

## KPI Calculation Details

All salespeople metrics use `kpi_por_vendedor(df, obj_df, df_ant, fecha_desde, fecha_hasta)` which:

1. **Groups transactions** by `id_vendedor`
2. **Aggregates** sales, transaction count, unique customers
3. **Calculates fulfillment** by joining with `objetivos_df` on `id_vendedor` and period
4. **Compares to prior period** by calculating delta from `df_ant`
5. **Assigns traffic light** based on fulfillment thresholds:
   - `label_semaforo = 'success'` if fulfillment ≥ 90%
   - `label_semaforo = 'warning'` if 70% ≤ fulfillment < 90%
   - `label_semaforo = 'danger'` if fulfillment < 70%
6. **Calculates ranking** by sorting on total sales descending

<Tip>
  The ranking position is dynamic — it updates based on your selected filters. This means you can see who's #1 in a specific zone or time period.
</Tip>
