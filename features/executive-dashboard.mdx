## Overview

The Executive Dashboard is your command center for sales intelligence. In seconds, you can see how your entire business is performing without opening a single spreadsheet.

<Note>
  The Executive Dashboard is the first screen you see when launching Visor KPI Comercial — it's designed for quick decision-making at the management level.
</Note>

## Key Visualizations

### Performance Gauge

A visual gauge shows your team's overall objective fulfillment at a glance. Below the gauge, you'll see:

- **🟢 Green:** Number of salespeople exceeding 90% of their quota
- **🟡 Yellow:** Salespeople between 70-90% of quota  
- **🔴 Red:** Salespeople below 70% requiring immediate attention

### Sales Evolution Chart

Track monthly sales trends over time with:
- Actual sales line (blue)
- Monthly objectives line (dotted red)
- Interactive tooltips showing exact values

<Tip>
  Hover over any point in the evolution chart to see detailed breakdowns including transaction count and average ticket size.
</Tip>

### Top Rankings

See your top performers across three dimensions:

**🏆 Top Salespeople**
- Sales volume
- Objective fulfillment %
- Traffic light status indicator
- Ranking position change vs. previous period

**🏪 Top Customers**
- Total purchase amount
- Purchase frequency
- Pareto category (A/B/C)
- Days since last purchase

**📦 Top Products**
- Sales volume by SKU
- Category classification
- Growth vs. previous period

## How KPIs Are Calculated

**Period Sales** (`kpi_ventas_periodo`)
- Sums all `importe_neto` from transactions in the selected date range
- Compares against sum from equivalent previous period
- Calculates fulfillment % against monthly objectives from `objetivos_df`

**Team Fulfillment** 
- Aggregates individual salesperson quotas
- Calculates weighted average based on each person's objective
- Traffic light thresholds: Green ≥90%, Yellow 70-90%, Red &lt;70%

**Gross Margin** (`kpi_margen`)
- Calculates `importe_neto - costo` for all transactions
- Computes margin % as `(margin / importe_neto) * 100`
- Compares total margin amount vs. previous period

**Customer Coverage** (`kpi_cobertura_clientes`)
- Counts unique `id_cliente` with transactions in period
- Divides by total active customers in `clientes_df`
- Identifies new customers (first purchase in period) and lost customers (no purchase in 60+ days)

<Tip>
  Use the sidebar filters to analyze specific time periods, sales zones, channels, or individual salespeople — all KPIs update instantly.
</Tip>
