## What Problem Does It Solve?

Many distribution companies still manage their sales data with multiple Excel files, manual reports, and hours of work spent consolidating data that arrives late, scattered, and error-prone.

The real problem isn't just the operational effort: **when data is scattered, making good business decisions becomes difficult and slow.**

**Visor KPI Comercial** solves exactly that:

- **Eliminates manual analysis** — converts Excel sales files into automatically processed KPIs
- **Centralizes business intelligence** — managers and sales reps access their metrics from a single dashboard
- **Anticipates problems** — an alert engine detects performance drops, at-risk customers, and growth opportunities before they impact results
- **Democratizes data** — each sales rep accesses their own individual report; management gets a consolidated view of the entire team

## Business Intelligence Capabilities

### Automated ETL Pipeline

Excel ingestion without manual configuration — the system reads, validates, and transforms sales files into a reproducible pipeline with pandas and openpyxl.

### KPI Engine

Automated calculation of critical business metrics:

- **Period sales** — Total revenue and segmentation
- **Gross margin** — Profitability by sales rep, customer, and product
- **Quota fulfillment** — % of target achieved vs monthly goal
- **Portfolio coverage** — % of active customers over total assigned
- **Period-over-period variance** — Absolute and relative delta MoM / YoY
- **Purchase frequency** — Recurrence by customer and channel
- **Product rotation** — Active vs inactive SKUs by period

### Pareto 80/20 Analysis

- Automatic classification of customers and products into **A / B / C** categories
- Calculation of portfolio **Gini concentration index**
- Identification of customers generating 80% of revenue
- Detection of low-rotation SKUs with potential stockout risk

### Intelligent Alert System

7 pre-configured business rules that trigger real-time alerts:

| ID | Alert | Severity |
|---|---|---|
| ALT001 | Sales rep with performance < 70% | Critical |
| ALT002 | Customer without purchases in last 30 days | High |
| ALT003 | Product with sales drop > 30% MoM | High |
| ALT004 | Customer portfolio with excessive concentration (Gini > 0.6) | Medium |
| ALT005 | Margin below minimum threshold | Critical |
| ALT006 | Portfolio coverage < 60% | High |
| ALT007 | Opportunity: customer with high frequency and low ticket | Opportunity |

## Technology Stack

Built with modern Python tools for data analysis and visualization:

- **Python 3.10+** — Runtime
- **Streamlit 1.32** — Multi-page UI framework
- **Plotly 5.19** — Interactive visualizations
- **Pandas 2.2** — ETL pipeline and data analysis
- **NumPy 1.26** — Numerical calculations
- **openpyxl** — Excel reading/writing
- **pytest 8.0** — Automated testing suite
